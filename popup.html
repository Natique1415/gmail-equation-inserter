Gmail Equation Inserter

LaTeX equations in your emails

How to use
1
Open Gmail and click Compose (or reply to an email)
2
Click the ∑ Insert Equation button in the formatting toolbar
3
Use Visual mode to click symbols, or LaTeX mode to type directly
4
Click Insert Equation — it appears inline in your email
Tips
✦ Equations are sent as images — recipients see them without any extensions
✦ Use the size slider to match your email's font size
✦ In Visual mode, click a structure first then fill in the slots
